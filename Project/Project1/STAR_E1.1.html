E1.1 Use the STAR aligner to map the RNA-seq reads to the reference genome. Compare the results with those from HISAT2, in terms of the numbers or percentages of reads uniquely aligned to the reference genome for each sequencing library. Note

STAR binaries for Linux and Mac can be directly downloaded from https://github.com/alexdobin/STAR/releases/download/2.7.11a/STAR_2.7.11a.zip

Using Star:

Make an index:

mkdir star_genome_index STAR –runThreadN 4 –runMode genomeGenerate –genomeDir star_genome_index
–genomeFastaFiles dmel-all-chromosome-r6.53.fasta
–sjdbGTFfile dmel-all-r6.53.gtf
